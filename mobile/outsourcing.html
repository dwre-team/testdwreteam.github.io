Outsourcing model in Software Development implies that Company that owns the Product, or that is responsible for Project Execution, hires the Vendor for full or partial Project Implementation.

Vendor, or in other words Technological Partner, takes responsibilities for Project Implementation, what implies full control over the development process and acceptance the obligation to make the whole Scope of work done in Time and Budget.
