Staff Augmentation is commonly used nowadays and many companies apply it to extend existing teams, or to fully transit the work to remote teams, though preserving full technological controll on the working process.

Like any other approach or methodology, Staff Augmentation can be the best choice in one case, while in another one there may be prefferable some other approach. Let's higlight Advantages and Diasdvantages of Staff Augmentation approach, to align the expectations to it.

Advantages of the Staff Augmentation approach:

  • Full transparency of Compensation mechanizm applied to Software Engineers by the Partner
  • Full transparency and control over Software Engineers work load
  • Hiring-Retention-Replacement-Dismissal functions can be fully or partially on the Partner
  • Jurisdictial aspects of work organization in offshore location are fully on the Partner
  • Wide technological and professional ranges
  • Optimal rates

Disadvantages of the Staff Augmentation approach:

  • Partner, as a Company with its own expertise and vision, cannot share best it has to support Vendor in Project's Implementation, since it is not directly involved into the Project
  • Technological risks are on the Vendor, Partner is a Support Services Provider

dwreTeam will do our best to utilize all Advantages of Staff Augmentation approach and help you in your journey to success.
