Outsourcing Software Development model presents the following advantages:

  • Technological side of Project implementation is on the Partner, what allows Company to concentrate on other things - Business, Marketing, Sales
  • Allows to use the whole technological potential of the Vendor, as it is directly involved into Project implementation
  • Cost effective, since one of Vendor's main functions is an effective operating of Human Resources what results in reduced rates one the Engineering Specialists for the Company

Disadvantages of the approach:

  • Outsourcing approach requires clear distributing of the managerial responsibilities on Company's side as it competes with an onsite development in terms of many functions

Contact us if you have any questions about Outsourcing your project and advantages such approach provides.
